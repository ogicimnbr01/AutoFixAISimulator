How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Auto Fix account, gameplay progress, and personal data, you have two options. In-app deletion is available for linked Google or Apple accounts. Anonymous guest accounts cannot be deleted from inside the app because they are used to maintain gameplay state, energy limits, and fairness protections.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest and most secure way to delete your data is directly from the app after your account is linked to Google or Apple.

  1. Open Auto Fix AI Simulator on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile Tab.
  3. Tap the Settings (Gear Icon) in the top right.
  4. If your account is linked to Google or Apple, scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account (Hesabı Sil).

Your data will be deleted immediately. If you continue using the app afterward, a fresh anonymous guest profile may be created.

Option 2: Web / Email Request

If you no longer have the app installed, or if you use an anonymous guest account, you can submit a manual deletion request via email.

Please send an email from the address associated with your account, containing your in-game User ID (if known) and a clear statement requesting account deletion. For anonymous guest accounts, include the in-game User ID when available so we can identify the correct data.

Send Deletion Request Email

What happens when you request deletion?

  • What Data is Deleted: All of your personal data (User ID, Display Name), gameplay progress, leaderboard rankings, energy, hint credits, and chat histories will be permanently deleted from our active systems.
  • What Data is Kept: Short-lived pseudonymous anti-abuse records that do not contain chat content may be retained temporarily to prevent unfair resetting of game limits.
  • Timeframe: Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days of submitting your request.
