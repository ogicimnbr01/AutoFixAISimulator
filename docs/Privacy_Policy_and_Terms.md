# Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

Thank you for playing **Auto Fix AI Simulator** ("we," "our," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** When you sign in (via Google, Apple, or Anonymously), we collect basic profile data (Display Name, User ID) through Firebase Authentication.
- **Gameplay Data:** We track your in-game progress, energy levels, hint usage, and diagnostic chat history to provide gameplay features like leaderboards and session persistence.
- **Chat Data (AI Interaction):** The text you send in the chat interface is processed by our servers (AWS) and sent to **AWS Bedrock (Amazon Nova Lite model)** to generate realistic, game-specific responses. **Please do not share any personal, sensitive, or real-world identifying information in the chat.** Your chat data is processed solely within the game's context and is **NOT used to train or fine-tune any AI model.**
- **Analytics:** We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (e.g., app crashes, feature usage) to improve the app.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
- To operate and maintain the game (e.g., saving your progress).
- To process your in-app purchases through RevenueCat.
- To generate AI chat responses based strictly on the game's mechanical scenarios.
- To maintain the global leaderboard.

### 3. Third-Party AI Disclosure
This application uses **Amazon Web Services (AWS) Bedrock** with the **Amazon Nova Lite** large language model to power its in-game AI chat mechanic. All AI-generated text is limited to simulated automotive diagnostic scenarios. AWS Bedrock operates under AWS's data processing terms, and your chat input is **not retained by AWS for model improvement or training purposes.** We do not use any other third-party AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, Google AI).

### 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share necessary data with:
- **AWS (Amazon Web Services / Bedrock):** To process the AI chat securely. AWS processes your chat prompts solely to generate a response and does not use them for model training.
- **Google / Apple / RevenueCat:** For authentication and processing in-app purchases.
- **AdMob (Google):** To provide optional rewarded advertisements.

### 5. Your Rights
You can request deletion of your account and associated data directly within the app's Profile settings or by contacting us at [Support Email].
